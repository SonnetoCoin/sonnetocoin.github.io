How to buy?

  1. Access PancakeSwap:

    • Go to https://pancakeswap.finance, a decentralized exchange on Binance Smart Chain.

  2. Connect Your Wallet:

    • Click on Connect Wallet.
    • Select your wallet (e.g., MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, Binance Wallet).
    • Follow the prompts to connect your wallet.

  3. Ensure Your Wallet Has BNB:

    • You need BNB (BEP-20) to trade for SONN and pay transaction fees.
    • If you don't have BNB, you can buy it from an exchange (e.g., Binance) and transfer it to your wallet.
    • Alternatively, some wallets, like Trust Wallet, also allow you to buy BNB directly within the app using a credit card or other payment methods.

  4. Import SONN:

    • In the swap screen, select BNB as the "From" currency.
    • Select SONNO as the "To" currency. If SONN isn't listed, paste the contract address below:
      • 0xea8d293ebc4b62359cb14d02bf50bb9c33c739cd

  5. Set Swap Amount and Confirm:

    • Enter the amount of BNB you wish to swap for SONN.
    • Adjust the slippage tolerance to 10%.
    • Click Swap and approve the transaction in your wallet.

Important Tips:

  • Ensure your wallet is on the Binance Smart Chain network.
  • Always double-check the contract address to avoid scams.
